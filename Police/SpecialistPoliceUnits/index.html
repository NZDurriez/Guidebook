Specialist Police Units

Specialist Police Units include:
Dogs (Delta), Air Support Unit (Eagle), Maritime (Water), Armed Offenders Squad (AOS), and Police Negotiation Team (PNT)

  • Dogs (Delta)
  • Air Support Unit (Eagle)
  • Maritime (Water)
  • Armed Offenders Squad (AOS)
  • Police Negotiation Team (PNT)

Delta and Eagle do not require applications. However, if you want a Delta callsign you must apply through the #fivepd-callsign-requests Discord channel.

Delta / Dog Commands
  • /dog – Spawns in/removes a dog
  • /dogx – Cancels your dog's current emote
  • /dsit – Makes your dog return to you and sit
  • /dlay – Makes your dog return to you and lie down
  • /dtrack <id> – Makes your dog track an offender
  • /dctrack – Stops dog tracking
  • /dhold – Holds the dog between your legs
  • /dbark – Makes the dog bark
  • /dheel – Commands the dog to come off the lead and heel
  • /dtp – Teleports your dog to you
  • /dcar – Makes your dog exit/enter your car
  • /dattack – Commands your dog to attack the player you are aiming at
Armed Offenders Squad (AOS) / Police Negotiation Team (PNT)

AOS and PNT are the only Specialist Units that require an application to join. You cannot roleplay or act as a member of these units unless you are accepted.

Applications:

  • Armed Offenders Squad (AOS):
    Apply via FivePD AOS Application
  • Police Negotiation Team (PNT):
    Apply by Creating A Ticket and submitting an in-character ticket.
