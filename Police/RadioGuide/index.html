Using the Radio

You'll need to be on the Police Radio to communicate and respond to events happening.

1. Activating your Radio

  1. Hit T on your keyboard to open your chat.
  2. Type /radio then press Enter.

Your radio should now appear in the bottom right of your screen.

2. Set Up Your Channel & Callsign

  1. Click the power button at the top right of the radio to turn it On/Off.
  2. Click within the field showing 0.00, type 1.00 and press Enter.
  3. Tap the right arrow twice to move to the callsign field, enter your callsign, and press Enter.
  4. Press Esc or type /radio again to put your radio away.
  5. Hold Left Alt to transmit over the radio (This can be remapped in your FiveM Keybinds).

Hover over each area for its function.
